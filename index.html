American Sign Language - Image Processing

Detects and classifies american sign language

This data science project leverages Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and the ResNet50 architecture to enhance the recognition and interpretation of American Sign Language (ASL). By using a diverse dataset of ASL gestures, the project aims to improve communication tools for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community, ensuring more accurate and real-time translation of sign language into text. The model's deep learning framework is particularly adept at handling complex visual patterns, promising significant advancements in both the accessibility and inclusivity of communication technologies.

Critical Economic Trends and Their Interrelationships

DV Project

This data visualization project titled "Critical Economic Trends and Their Interrelationships" employs cosine similarity to analyze and present interconnected economic indicators in an intuitive format using Tableau. By mapping the relationships between various economic metrics such as GDP growth, unemployment rates, and inflation, the project visualizes how closely these trends align or diverge across different regions and times. This approach provides policymakers, economists, and academic scholars with a clear, interactive representation of economic dynamics, enabling them to discern patterns and correlations that are crucial for informed decision-making and forecasting. The use of Tableau enhances the accessibility and engagement of the visualizations, making complex economic relationships easier to understand and analyze.
